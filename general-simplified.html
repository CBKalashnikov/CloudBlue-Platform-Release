  • CBPFR-1048 Performance improvements.
  • CBPFR-640 A new status was added for subscriptions: Lapsed unexpectedly. It will be assigned to subscriptions that were configured to be renewed but were not renewed on time for an unknown reason. This status prevents subscriptions from unexpected endings and undesired deletions. Administrators can use this status to identify such subscriptions, find the reason why they were not renewed, fix it, and keep the subscriptions alive.
    This feature is enabled by default, and there is no setting to turn it off.
  • CBPFR-368 Now, if a cancellation order is failed with an error and it was canceled, the subscription's state will be correctly restored in the OSS.
  • CBPFR-641 Subscriptions that are being renewed can no longer be modified until the process is completed. This prevents discrepancies between their state on the vendor and CloudBlue Commerce sides.
